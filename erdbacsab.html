Erdős-Bacon-Sabbath Number

The following quantities are relatively well-known:
  • Erdős Number: Steps to Paul Erdős through co-authored publications.
  • Bacon Number: Steps to Kevin Bacon through acting in the same film.
The sum of these two quantities is the Erdős-Bacon Number. Another quantity is slightly less well-known:
  • Sabbath Number: Steps to Black Sabbath through being in the same musical performance.
These three are combined to form the Erdős-Bacon-Sabbath Number. Jonathan Love has an Erdős-Bacon-Sabbath Number of 11, as can be confirmed by the following data:
