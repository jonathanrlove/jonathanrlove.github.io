Projects

Members of MATH 377 (Honours Number Theory) completed a 10-15 page paper on a topic of their choice. Here is a collection of some of the completed projects.
  • Adam Benzaari. Number Theory in the Islamic World.
  • Helena Heinonen. Inventing New Numbers: an exploration of the p-adics.
  • Zack Horton. Computing Zeroes of the Riemann Zeta Function.
  • Jun Kai Liao. Dirichlet Unit Theorem.
  • Taha Rhaouti. Transcendence of e and π.
  • Avram Silberztein. The theory of partitions.
  • Bronwyn Walsh. Farey Diagrams.
  • Anwyn Woodyatt. An Introduction to Gröbner Bases.
  • Augustin Wright. On the Multiplicity of the Ramanujan τ function.
